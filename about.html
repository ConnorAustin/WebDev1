Founders

In 2015 to Lipscomb college Sophomores met and bonded over their love of charities. They then descided to build a crowdfunding platform to help fund themselves and other social entrepreneurs.

Where We Come In

Here at HOPE NOW! Our vision is to empower and connect capable social entrepreneurs in order to help them address problems in their society.

We aid their mission by first helping them to create a coherent strategy then give them a platform to campaign for their cause.

Process

Step 1

Someone finds a problem that they want to solve.

Step 2

They campaign on our crowdfunding platform.

Step 3

Our community of volunteers vote on the solutions they find viable.
